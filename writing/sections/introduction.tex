\section{Introduction}

With most of the discourse on America's wealthiest being centered on inequality and the harmful effects of wealth concentration,
there is little input on the potential positive effects of having wealthy people in an area. In this study, we attempt to see if the wealthy have a measurably 
positive impact on their communities through a variety of channels. Most notably through economic benefits like job opportunities, environmental benefits, as well as tax and charitable contributions.
In doing so, we hope to show that wealth concentration is a more nuanced topic than what most discussions appeal to.
